Legends

∼Barcelona∼ Once upon a time Jason wanted to go on a journey, he asked Hera to predict a quiet journey without Many difficulties. Hera, as a good friend of Jason, granted his wish, Jason asked for help from Heracles and Hermes to help him, they took 9 boats because of the difficulties they could find on their way, When they had been traveling for a while, they realized that they were missing a boat. Heracles and Hermes went in search of that boat, after a while, they found that boat docked in a port, Curious about where they had found that boat, they decided to visit that unknown city, they walked through its streets, they met its people... They fell in love with that city so much that they decided call it...Barcanona, in honor of the ninth boat that was lost.

-Cazadores de Hermes
